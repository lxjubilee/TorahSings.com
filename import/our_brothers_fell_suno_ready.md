## GENERATION TIPS

1. Set mode to **Custom**
2. Paste **Style Prompt** → Style of Music field
3. Toggle **Exclude Styles** ON → paste exclusions
4. Paste **Lyrics** into Lyrics field
5. Title: **Our Brothers Fell**
6. Generate **3–4 variations** → pick the heaviest emotional build

**If Suno truncates:**
- **Part 1:** Spoken Intro through end of first Chorus
- **Part 2:** Verse 2 through Outro

**Key moments:**
- "Pulled our brothers down the stairs" — the emotional knife; these are the singers' own kind
- "And we watched him lift an axe" — the pivot from despair to defiance
- "Wood on wood, plank on plank" — should hit rhythmically, like hammer strikes
- Outro parentheticals layer as echo choir beneath the final spoken line
- "The word 'good' was still breathing" — needs silence around it

**If generations feel too soft:** Add "war drums, tribal percussion" to the style prompt.
