## ✦ FIELD 3: LYRICS
*(Paste everything below into the Lyrics field)*

```
[Spoken Intro]
We heard Him walking.
We always heard Him walking in the garden,
The rhythm of the cool of the day.
But this time… they were hiding.

[Verse 1]
The Foot came through the garden,
The same step that stirred the deep.
But the ones He walked toward were crouching,
Sewn in leaves and sick with shame.

The serpent lost its standing,
Pressed face-down into the dirt.
A heel would bleed, a head would shatter,
The first wound and the first word.

[Pre-Chorus]
And we who sang when the foundations formed,
Now watched the foundations shake.

[Chorus]
The garden broke from the inside,
The serpent hid inside the good.
The basket cracked, the beauty twisted,
And nothing held the way it should.
Tov, tov, the word still echoed,
But the hands that reached had changed.
The garden broke from the inside,
And the walking turned to shame.

[Verse 2]
And she saw, and she reached,
And she took, and she tasted.
And she gave, and he ate,
And the chain was not wasted.

Link by link the nails were driven,
Sight to longing, reach to fall.
The same word that built the heavens,
Became the word that chained them all.

[Pre-Chorus]
And we who watched the Nail pin sky to earth,
Now watched it pin the cage shut.

[Chorus]
The garden broke from the inside,
The serpent hid inside the good.
The basket cracked, the beauty twisted,
And nothing held the way it should.
Tov, tov, the word still echoed,
But the hands that reached had changed.
The garden broke from the inside,
And the walking turned to shame.

[Bridge]
But before He drove them out,
He killed. He cut. He stitched.
He wrapped their skin in something borrowed,
A covering they did not earn.

The great basket shattered… 
But a smaller one was sewn.
Goodness didn't die that evening,
It narrowed to a thread,
And followed them into the dust.

[Outro]
And then He looked at us.
And we took our places at the gate.
The sword, the flame, the turning blade.

(We who sang at the foundations)
(Now guarded what remained)

The garden closed behind them.
And we… stood… still.

[End]
```

## GENERATION TIPS

1. Set mode to **Custom**
2. Paste **Style Prompt** → Style of Music field
3. Toggle **Exclude Styles** ON → paste exclusions
4. Paste **Lyrics** into Lyrics field
5. Title: **The Garden Broke**
6. Generate **3–4 variations** → pick the one with the heaviest emotional weight

**If Suno truncates:**
- **Part 1:** Spoken Intro through end of first Chorus
- **Part 2:** Verse 2 through Outro

**Key moments to listen for in your generations:**
- "Tov, tov" in the chorus — should hit like a tolling bell or heartbeat
- "And she saw, and she reached, and she took, and she tasted" — should feel rhythmic, relentless, like chain links snapping
- "He killed. He cut. He stitched." — short percussive phrases, the emotional turn of the whole song
- "And we… stood… still." — needs silence around it; the ellipses create pauses

**To strengthen the choir:**
Add `[Choir]` before the Chorus lines, or duplicate key lines in parentheses for echo layering.
