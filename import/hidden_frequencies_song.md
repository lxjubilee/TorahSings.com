## SONG STRUCTURE & LYRICS

**[Intro — Orchestral Swell, Single Sustained Note]**

*(Spoken, low and resonant over a single vibrating string tone:)*
Before the first word was written,
there was a sound.

---

**[Verse 1 — Minor Key, Strings Only, Measured and Building]**

Three notes make a chord,
Six strings sing the overtone,
Nine returns to where it started —
Every cycle finds its home.

A frequency was buried
Beneath the ancient ink,
A melody inside the letters
Older than we think.

---

**[Pre-Chorus — Brass Enters, Tempo Lifts]**

The cantillation marks still whisper
What the scribes already knew —
Every word was meant for singing,
Every verse a song born through.

---

**[Chorus — Full Orchestra + Choir, Triumphant Major Key]**

There are hidden frequencies
Woven in the sacred page,
Songs that shook the foundations
Long before we built the stage.

Three, and six, and nine —
The numbers never lied.
The voice of the Creator
Is the sound we carry inside.

---

**[Verse 2 — Cello Lead, Intimate Then Expanding]**

A string vibrates in ratios —
The octave, fifth, and fourth.
Physics didn't invent the pattern,
It discovered what came forth.

The Torah wasn't only read,
It rang through desert air.
The psalms were not just poetry —
They were frequencies of prayer.

---

**[Pre-Chorus — Building Percussion, Driving Forward]**

From Fibonacci spirals turning
In the chambers of a shell,
To the rhythm of the heavens —
There's a song the numbers tell.

---

**[Chorus — Full Orchestra + Choir, Even Bigger]**

There are hidden frequencies
Woven in the sacred page,
Songs that shook the foundations
Long before we built the stage.

Three, and six, and nine —
The numbers never lied.
The voice of the Creator
Is the sound we carry inside.

---

**[Bridge — Time Signature Shifts to 3/4, Waltz-Like, Ethereal Choir]**

*(Softer, wonder-filled:)*

Two to one — the octave breathes,
Three to two — the fifth rings clear,
Every ratio a fingerprint
Of the One who engineers.

What if every verse of scripture
Holds a note we've yet to hear?
What if heaven's been composing
And the song is finally here?

---

**[Final Chorus — Key Change Up, Maximum Intensity, Choir in Unison]**

There are hidden frequencies
Blazing through the sacred page,
Songs that cracked the foundations,
Songs that will outlast the age!

Three, and six, and nine —
The patterns never end.
The voice of Yahuah thunders
In every vibrating strand!

---

**[Outro — Orchestra Pulls Back to Single Note, Then Silence]**

*(Spoken, quiet, reverent:)*
In the beginning was the Word.
And the Word… was a sound.

*(Single sustained tone fades.)*
