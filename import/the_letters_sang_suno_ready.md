## GENERATION TIPS

**Before you hit Create:**
1. Set mode to **Custom**
2. Paste **Style Prompt** into the Style of Music field
3. Toggle **Exclude Styles** ON → paste the exclusions
4. Paste **Lyrics** into the Lyrics field (everything inside the code block above)
5. Title: **The Letters Sang**
6. Generate **3–4 variations** → pick the strongest orchestral build

**If Suno truncates (song too long):**
Split into two generations —
- **Part 1:** Spoken Intro through end of first Chorus
- **Part 2:** Verse 2 through Outro
Use "Continue" or re-paste with the second half

**Vocal notes:**
- The `[Spoken Intro]` and `[Outro]` tags tell Suno to deliver those lines as speech, not sung
- Parentheses in the Outro `(We were there)` suggest background/echo vocal layering
- The single-word lines "Three." and "Six." in the Pre-Chorus sections create dramatic pauses
- "Vav, Vav, Vav" in Verse 2 — the commas create rhythmic separation, almost percussive
- "Tov... Good." in the Bridge — the ellipsis creates a sustained pause before "Good"

**If you want to push the choir harder on the Chorus:**
Add `[Choir]` tag before the Chorus lyrics, or add `(the signature of Elohim)` as a parenthetical echo line after the main Chorus line to trigger backup vocals.
