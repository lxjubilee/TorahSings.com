## 1. Method — how candidates were chosen and what "Paleo-Hebrew supports it" means

The whole catalog is **already** sung in the celestial "we" of Job 38:7 — the two voices being **the sons of Elohim (the Angels)** and **the morning stars (the Picture Letters)**. So this pass did **not** change any singing voice; it makes the singer **explicit** more often, so a listener is reminded *who* "we" is.

A song qualified only when **all** held:
1. **Angel-voiced (or interwoven), not pure-Letters.** A song sung by the Picture Letters ("I am the nail") must *not* say "we the angels."
2. **Textual / Paleo-Hebrew support = the derivation genuinely places the angelic host in the scene** (the cherubim, the Angel of Yahuah, the divine council, the chariots/host, Job 38:7, the seraphim, the ministering/gathering angels). Rated HIGH / MED per edit.
3. **Under-named in the sung lines** — a bare "we watched/stood/saw" that never names the singers, OR self-naming that appears only once in a bridge while the chorus/refrain stays generic ("**promote** to a more-sung line").

**Curation rules applied on the widen pass (to protect the songs):**
- **Add** naming to generic "we" lines; **promote** bridge-only naming into a chorus/pre-chorus/outro line.
- **Do NOT convert an existing Picture-Letters line into an angel line** (that swaps one celestial voice for another rather than widening) — several Psalms candidates were skipped on this ground.
- **Do NOT rewrite a deliberate final-silence line** (the ellipsis closers written to land in silence) or replace a load-bearing Scripture image.

**Release gates re-checked on every edit (all pass):** First-Person Referent (I/we/us/our = Angels/Letters only; the singular Angel of Yahuah keeps His own "I/My"; the lying spirit / destroying Angel stay witnessed); Narrative-time (no NT hindsight in OT songs); the **Genesis-6 trap** (there "sons of Elohim" = the *fallen* — "the angels/watchers/heaven's host" used instead); Suno limits (≤5000; Styles untouched); OHI/sacred names unchanged.

**Survey:** two waves of parallel read-only agents across all 66 book folders; the main agent applied every edit. Key finding: **the catalog already self-names heavily wherever Scripture most demands it** (Luke 2 Gloria, Isaiah 6 seraphim in the bridge, the empty-tomb angels, the Psalms of the hosts, Job 38 Track 1, the cherubim psalms) — those were left alone or only lightly *promoted*.

## 5. Deliberately NOT changed (representative)

- **Already-saturated** (the model to imitate): "Light Before the Sun" ("we hung there, the sons of Elohim"); "Dead, He Began to Speak" ("we, the watchers"); Joshua 5 "Neither For You, Nor For Them"; 2 Sam 24 destroying angel ("I, the destroying one"); 1 Kg 8 "we are the cherubim"; the annunciation/Gloria and empty-tomb NT songs; the Psalms of the hosts (91/99/103/148/150/68/18); Job 38 Track 1.
- **Pure Picture-Letters voice — wrong for angel self-naming:** "The Second Day Undone" ("we were the letters that sang the split"); Judges 5 "The Stars Fought From Their Courses"; the Proverbs acrostic songs; Genesis/Exodus Letter-lead tracks. Would take "we, the Picture Letters," never "we, the angels."
- **Deliberately skipped on the widen pass to protect the songs:** any edit that would *convert a Picture-Letters line* into an angel line (e.g. Ps 29/33/34 promotes), *rewrite a final-silence ellipsis line*, or *replace a load-bearing Scripture image* (e.g. Isa 24 "windows from on high," Isa 52 tripled "watchmen," Ecc 11 "the sower trusted," Jer 6 "peace, peace"); the benediction track "Then Dust Called the Name" (its we=angels ÷ they=dust final chorus is a hard gate); Song of Songs 3 (Zayin/swords) and Isaiah 62 (recasting Yahuah's "I have set watchmen").
