## Executive Summary

**Same DNA, different organism.** *The Letters Sang* is the **prototype** — a one-pass, single-author proof that songs live inside the text, written in the founding conversation before any governing standard existed. *Light Before the Sun* is a **production song** — the same 3-6-9 engine, now running under six layers of governance that did not exist when the prototype was written. Six deltas explain every difference:

1. **The subject changed.** *The Letters Sang* is a song **about the cipher** — its chorus is literally the method ("A Foot, a Nail, a Basket closing, / Three, and Six, and Nine"). *Light Before the Sun* is a song **about the passage**, powered by the cipher underneath — its chorus is a theological claim ("The sun does not make the light — the sun hangs inside the light… and every lamp since is only borrowing"). The numbers moved out of the chorus and into isolated frame-declarations.
2. **The Letters changed roles.** In the prototype the angels *watch* the letters ("We watched the letters take their places"). In the new song the letters *sing* — the two-voices correction (the morning stars of Job 38:7 **are** the Letters) landed between the two songs. "Every letter waiting to be called… with every word, one more voice rising" is that doctrine turned into vocal architecture.
3. **The evidence bar rose from arithmetic to orthography.** The prototype's proofs are counts (day 3, day 6, seven *tov*s). The new song's proofs are single-letter Masoretic facts: the one-Vav difference of Genesis 1:3, and *me'orot* spelled defective — checkable in any critical edition.
4. **The scope narrowed by law.** The prototype spans Genesis 1–2 as one song — which the signed methodology now forbids (two narrative units → two songs). *Light Before the Sun* covers exactly 1:1–2:3; Genesis 2 became its own track (*Our Maker Knelt in Dust*).
5. **The music changed constitutions.** Prototype: Western Broadway, D minor → D major, "Hans Zimmer meets Andrew Lloyd Webber." New song: **Adonai Malach mode on D**, cantillation, kinnor, shofar, frame drum — the Hebraic Music Mandate, plus album-level harmonic architecture (its bright mode exists so Track 3's fall into Ahavah Rabbah *is* the crack).
6. **Quality moved from inspiration to adversarial verification** — provably: a line-class the prototype itself contains (untraceable "mountains") was drafted again by one of the new song's writers **and killed by the judge.**

## The Evidence

**1. The chorus swap — method vs. meaning.**
Prototype chorus: *"We watched the letters take their places, / Nailed into the breath of Yah. / A Foot, a Nail, a Basket closing, / Three, and Six, and Nine. / The signature of Elohim…"* — a catalog of the cipher. If sixty-one songs carried this chorus formula, the catalog would be one song sung sixty-one times. The new song's chorus carries a claim only Genesis 1 can make — which is why the forge ran a **signature check** explicitly banning the prototype's lines, and why every song in the catalog gets its own theological anchor.

**2. The killed lines — the smoking gun.**
The prototype contains, at verified lines of the shipped file (`the_letters_sang_suno_ready.md`):
- Line 37: *"Before the mountains learned to sleep"* — no mountains exist in Genesis 1–2; the image drifts in from Psalm 90:2.
- Line 63: *"Shaped from dust but filled with flame"* — Genesis 2:7 gives **breath**, not flame.
- Line 82: *"Seven times He spoke the word"* — the formula is *vayar* — **He saw.**

The new song's audit log records: Draft B wrote *"They sang before the mountains stood"* — **killed** as a Psalm 90:2 import. Draft A wrote *"He called it tov"* — **corrected** to *"He saw it — tov."* Draft A wrote *"no burning wheel"* — **killed**, no footing in Genesis 1. The exact flaw-classes the prototype carries were regenerated by the new writers — drift is constant — **and the adversarial layer caught every one.** That single observation is the difference between the two processes.

**3. The perspective evolution, in one pair of lines.**
Prototype: *"We watched the letters take their shape"* — the letters are scenery.
New song: *"Every letter waiting to be called… With every word, one more voice rising"* — the letters are the choir, entering one wave per creative word. The two-voices doctrine is not stated in the song; it is *staged* in it.

**4. The evidence-bar delta, in one pair of discoveries.**
Prototype payload: *tov* = Tet+Vav+Bet, "the basket that secures the house" — beautiful, but a pictographic lens (devotional, per the caution note). New payload: *yehi or* → *vayehi-or* differ by one Vav; *me'orot* is written without any — **"The lamps are hung… the light was nailed."** A skeptic can verify both in BHS in under a minute. The platform invites skeptics; the songs got harder to argue with.

**5. What was deliberately preserved — the DNA.**
The skeleton (Spoken Intro → verses → one-word pre-chorus declarations → bridge → echo-parenthetical outro → silence), the *Three. / Six. / Nine.* device, the explicit-numbers reveal mode, the caution posture, and D as tonal center — all inherited from the prototype, because the Engine was reverse-engineered *from* it. The prototype is not superseded; it is the ancestor every governing document was written to industrialize.
