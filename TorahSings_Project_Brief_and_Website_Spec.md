## 1. The Vision

Torah Sings is a discovery platform built on a single, striking premise: **there are songs hidden inside the Scriptures themselves.** When the Paleo-Hebrew text is taken symbol by symbol and a numerical/symbolic sequence is applied to it, melodies emerge — and these songs read as being sung from the *angelic* perspective rather than the human one. This is not us singing up to heaven; it is heaven's own song surfacing out of the structure of the text.

This is **proven, not theoretical.** Actual song productions have already been generated from the decoded Paleo-Hebrew. The platform's job is to unveil that discovery to the world in a way that is experiential, educational, and reverent — and to do so as its own dedicated revelation stream, distinct from the broader Jubilee Music catalog.

**One-line positioning:**
> *Fragments of a song scattered through the Scriptures — now unveiled.*

**Why its own website:** This discovery is significant enough that it deserves to stand alone rather than get lost in the wider media mesh. Torah Sings is a separate theological and aesthetic experience with its own identity, its own audience journey, and its own economics.

## 2. Theological Foundation

The angels-and-stars-singing theme is the spiritual spine of the entire platform, and it is anchored in Scripture:

- **Job 38:7** — the morning stars sang together, and the sons of Elohim shouted for joy at creation.
- The thread of **celestial worship** runs throughout the Scriptures and is largely untapped in modern faith media.

**Framing (critical):** The platform never claims canon status. The posture is:

> *We are brushing away tradition and human thinking and going back to the ancient Hebrew — letting the Scriptures speak for themselves from a fresh perspective. This is not presented as canon. It is presented as something to consider.*

This "biblical archaeology from a linguistics perspective" framing keeps the project both humble and provocative. It invites the curious without overclaiming.

**Persona positioning:** The Inspire Family are presented as *vessels and interpreters* of the angelic song — not its originators. The stars sing; the angels sing; the Inspire Family gives that heavenly song an earthly voice.

**Style & Naming Conventions (internal):**
- Default to OHI conventions: **Yahuah, Yeshua, Elohim, Ruach HaKodesh** (feminine).
- Hebrew article rule: never "the Ruach HaKodesh" — use "Ruach HaKodesh" or "the Ruach Kodesh." Same principle for HaMashiach, HaTorah, etc.
- "OHI" / "CCI" are **internal labels only** — they never appear in reader-facing copy.

## 3. The Three-Prong Architecture

The website is one unified platform with three interconnected pathways. Together they move a visitor from **experience → knowledge → capability.**

### Prong 1 — Torah Sings *(Experiential — the Home)*
The entry point. Angels and stars singing, hidden in the Scriptures. This is where people arrive, feel the mystery, and hit play. Music-forward, wonder-forward.

### Prong 2 — Hebraic Christianity *(Doctrinal — the Deepening)*
Articles (read aloud) that explain ancient Hebraic concepts, principles, and the deeper "secrets" and gems hidden in the Hebrew Scriptures. The revelation expands from *song* to *understanding*. Songs are only the entry point — there are doctrinal treasures throughout the text.

> **Naming note:** Candidate names include "Hebraic Christianity," "Hebrew for New Testament Believers," and "Hebraic Roots of the Faith." Recommend **differentiating clearly** from the existing HebrewForChristians.com (see §9). "Hebraic Christianity" is the working primary.

### Prong 3 — Learn Hebrew *(Literacy — the Empowerment)*
An educational, genuinely fun on-ramp to the Hebraic origins of the Bible. Visitors don't need fluency — just enough literacy to begin discovering more for themselves. Voiced primarily by **Zev Inspire and Zariah Inspire** (already beta-tested and strong).

**Why the progression matters:** A visitor enters through *song*, is intellectually awakened by *articles*, then gains the actual *tools* to unlock hidden layers of Scripture on their own. This is a discipleship ecosystem disguised as a subscription service.

## 4. Content Model

### Album Structure
- **Seven songs per album** (deliberately — *not* the twelve-song Jubilee Music standard). Seven signals rarity, covenant, and sacred weight rather than mass production.
- **Topic-specific**, not book-specific and not artist-specific. Albums are organized around themes such as *Creation*, *The Covenant Promises*, etc. The album is the protagonist — not the persona.
- **Angel/star artwork theme** woven throughout, with visual *variety* across albums (different celestial scenes, different moments) so the motif stays iconic rather than repetitive.
- Projected library scale: potentially **~1,000 songs** over time — enough to sustain long-term engagement.

### Presenter Rotation
- The **twelve Inspire Family members rotate** presenting the album articles, keeping interpretive voices fresh across topics.
- **Gabriel-AI stays behind the scenes.** Front-line presentation belongs to the twelve.
- **Zev Inspire** is the overall persona in charge of the platform.

### The Album Detail Page — Three Tabs
Every album page serves three different audiences at once:

| Tab | Purpose | Audience |
|-----|---------|----------|
| **Article** | Devotional + informative. Encouragement that deepens the mystery. Includes a **read-aloud** button. | The devotional listener |
| **Lyrics** | The hidden text revealed — the words surfaced from the decoding. | The reader / worshipper |
| **Derivation** | Step-by-step explanation of how the song was derived from the Scripture, for the analytically minded and the skeptical. | The "show me how" thinker |

**On what to reveal:** Release enough of the methodology that people say *"Oh — that's how it was done. Fascinating."* The core algorithm and certain elements may stay proprietary/undisclosed, but the derivation must be substantial enough to earn credibility and provoke wonder. Yes, others could eventually attempt to copy the approach — but by then the discovery is already ours.

## 5. Website Design & UX

### Overall Aesthetic Direction
A **Netflix-style base** (hero section + visual album cards) — familiar and frictionless — but elevated with **mystique, secrecy, and reverence.** The goal is a balance: scholarly enough to teach, but immediate enough that someone who just wants to *experience* it can jump straight in without friction. Not everyone is scholarly; many want to hit play first and understand later. The design must honor both.

### Home Page Layout
1. **Hero section (top).** The primary hook. Draws people into the concept, the articles, the "how this works." Music can be played directly from here.
2. **Visual album cards (below).** Each card links to a topic album's detail page.
3. **Book link / funnel** further down — an offer to purchase the book that explains the full discovery and more.

### First-Load Intro Modal
- A **pop-up window launches on the visitor's first visit** with a **2–3 minute intro video** explaining the discovery.
- Tone: **secretive, mystical — "here's a secret."** The paradox of restraint: tell people not to share it, and they share it. We *want* it shared; the mystique framing drives organic spread.
- A **persistent "Replay Intro" link** lets returning visitors watch it again.

### Album Detail Pages
- Reached by clicking an album card.
- Contains the **three tabs** (Article / Lyrics / Derivation) described in §4.
- **Read-aloud** available on the Article tab.
- Playback available directly on the page.

### The "Secret" Psychology (design principle)
Everything leans into *discovered* rather than *marketed*. Forbidden/hidden knowledge spreads faster than promoted knowledge. Positioning, copy, artwork, and the intro video all reinforce: this is special, hidden, mysterious, and found in the Scriptures.

### Design Reference Points
- **From HebrewForChristians.com:** structural clarity — organized categories and progressive, layered learning.
- **From *The Secret* (Rhonda Byrne) — positioning only, not content:** the "fragments hidden through history, now revealed" framing and multi-touchpoint monetization (site + book). *We are not copying her work* — we are borrowing the revelation-marketing posture and applying it to a genuinely biblical, Paleo-Hebrew discovery.

## 7. Monetization

### Model: Yearly Subscription
- **Price: $87.95 / year.**
  - Intentionally specific (not a round number) so it reads as researched, not arbitrary.
  - High enough to signal real value; framed so it never feels like gouging or bait-and-switch.
- **Why yearly (not monthly, not one-time):** predictable revenue without the "support this forever" liability of a lifetime fee, and without the churn problem a monthly plan would face on a high-niche catalog. Yearly is the commitment window — and it positions subscribers as **partners in ongoing revelation**, not one-time buyers.

### Free Tier
A real taste that hooks without giving everything away — e.g., **2–3 full albums** (or limited daily access), then the rest gated. *(Exact free-tier mechanics to be finalized during build.)*

### The Reverse-Engineered Value Package
The price is justified by stacking value so the yearly plan feels like a steal:
- Full **Torah Sings** library access (current + all future albums).
- Full **Hebraic Christianity** article library (read-aloud).
- Full **Learn Hebrew** curriculum (Zev + Zariah).
- The **book(s)** explaining the discovery, included.
- **Early access** to new topic albums as they release.
- **Behind-the-scenes** / exclusive content.
- A downloadable **resources kit** (methodology at the disclosed level) for serious students.

### Positioning Language
> *Support the discovery. Fund the biblical archaeology. Access the treasury.*

As more is uncovered — new albums, deeper Hebraic insights, expanded Hebrew lessons — active subscribers automatically receive it. The subscription is an **investment in ongoing spiritual archaeology**, not merely a purchase.

## 9. Reference Research (Findings)

**HebrewForChristians.com**
- Traditional, clean information architecture: Hebrew Alphabet, Biblical Grammar, Holidays, Messianic Perspective, glossary, store.
- Strength: **structural clarity** and progressive learning.
- Gap for our purposes: visually dated, and it does **not** teach from a **Paleo-Hebrew** perspective — which is precisely our differentiator.

**The Secret (Rhonda Byrne)**
- Positions itself as scattered fragments of hidden knowledge, now revealed for the first time.
- Marketing leans hard into **mystery, discovery, revelation**, with monetization across book + film + merchandise.
- **We borrow the positioning posture, not the content**, and ground ours in Scripture and the Paleo-Hebrew discovery.

**Synthesis:** Take Byrne's *revelation-marketing language* + H4C's *structural clarity*, and express both through the three-prong model on a Netflix-style, mystique-elevated interface.
